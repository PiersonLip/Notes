    and \\ [8pt]
    $\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} a_n$ diverges if and only if $\int_1^\infty f(x) \, dx$ diverges. \\ [15pt]

    I) Must be continous for all values of x\\
    II) Must be positive\\
    III) Must be decreasing \\
